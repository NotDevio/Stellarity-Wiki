Modern Player-Friendly Guide

Armor

Champion Armor

Damage Scaling Combat Build Obtaining:
  • Altar of the Accursed
  • 4 Chorus Plating
  • 1 Enderite Smithing Template
  • Netherite armor piece
Set Bonus:
  • Each hit increases next attack damage by 6%, up to 5 stacks (~40%)
  • Stacks last 3 seconds
  • Hit sounds indicate stacks
  • Purple sprint trail (cosmetic)
Tips:
  • Works with almost any weapon
  • Best with fast attack builds
  • Stacks with Duskberry bonuses

Hallowed Armor

Mobility Survivability Obtaining:
  • Altar of the Accursed
  • 4 Hallowed Ingots
  • 1 Enderite Smithing Template
  • Netherite armor piece
Piece Bonuses:
  • Helmet & Chestplate: +1 safe fall distance
  • Leggings: +15% jump, +25% sneak speed
  • Boots: +10% movement speed, +0.5 step height
Full Set — Holy Protection:
  • Immune to damage for 5s after taking any damage
  • No knockback
  • Fire Protection + Water Breathing (8s)
  • Temporary speed boost
  • Cooldown: 25s
Tips:
  • Very versatile
  • Great for boss fights
  • Strong with mobility builds

Shulker Armor

Defense Summoner Style Obtaining:
  • Altar of the Accursed
  • 4 Shulker Shells
  • 1 Enderite Smithing Template
  • Netherite armor piece or defeat Shulking boss
Set Bonus:
  • 50% chance on damage to spawn friendly Shulker Bullets
  • Immune to Levitation & Wither
  • Resistance I when surrounded by 4+ mobs
Tips:
  • Great for crowd control
  • Use speed boosts to counter slow movement
  • Resistance replaces need for Endurance potions

Floral Armor

Ranged Build Bow Focused Obtaining:
  • Altar of the Accursed
  • 8 Cherry Leaves
  • 1 Enderite Smithing Template
  • Netherite armor piece
Set Bonuses:
  • +25% arrow velocity (Chestplate)
  • +10% arrow damage (Leggings)
  • -5% melee damage per piece (max -20%)
  • Arrows create explosive flowers
  • Consecutive hits increase explosion damage
  • Sneaking in tall plants grants invisibility (cosmetic)
Tips:
  • Best used with bows/crossbows
  • Not ideal for melee combat
  • Traditional arrows benefit most
