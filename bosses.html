Bosses

🌈 Empress of Light

Summoning

Spawned by throwing 1 Starlight Soot on an Altar of The Sacred in The End or Overworld.

  • 🌙 Night (End or Overworld at night) → Default Night version
  • ☀ Day (Overworld daytime) → Daylight version

Abilities

  • Dash
  • Ethereal Lance
  • Ethereal Lance II
  • Prismatic Bolts
  • Prismatic Bolts II
  • Teleport
  • Teleport (Particle Form)

Drops — Night Version

  • Prismatic Punch — 25%
  • Prismember — 25%
  • Starstruck Shield — 25%
  • Prismatic Pearl — 25%
  • Empress Wings — 10%
  • Soaring Insignia — 15%

Drops — Daytime Version

  • Kaleidoscope — 100%
  • Soaring Insignia — 100%
  • Empress Wings — 50%
  • Fluffy Hammer of Justice — 30%
