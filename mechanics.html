Mechanics & Crafting Guide

Altar of The Accursed

Altar of The Accursed

Mechanic Obtaining:
  • Spawns naturally in a pillar on the main island
  • Cannot be mined or picked up
Recipe:
  • Throw items on top to combine into new items via Altar recipes
Usage:
  • Used to craft new items
  • Disenchanting removed in newer versions

Cauldron Crafting

Crafting Usage:
  • Fill the Cauldron with Water and throw 1 Dragon's Breath into the Cauldron, you can craft up to 7 items before needing to throw another Dragon's Breath.

Armor Penetration

Definition: Armor Penetration (AP) allows a portion of damage to ignore armor calculations, dealing “true damage” or derivative true damage. Often shown as Hardcore hearts (HP♥♥). Useful for high-defense mobs or heavily armored players.

Consecration

Definition: Converts items into other items when dropped into Shimmer or water in The Hallow. If the item does not glow, it is likely incorrect.

Void Fishing

Definition: Fish in the Void by moving far from the central island (use End Gateway).
Mechanics: Wait 15–45 seconds for a catch; each Lure level deducts 5s from max wait. Fisher of Voids reduces min and max wait by 4s and 8s respectively.
