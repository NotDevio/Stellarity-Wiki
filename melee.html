Modern Player-Friendly Guide

Weapons

Tamaris

Support Execute Obtaining:

Obtaining:

  • Altar of the Accursed
  • 8 Enderite Shards
  • Enderite Smithing Template
  • Netherite Sword
  • Wither Skeleton Skull

Pre-v4.0.0: Dropped from Ender Dragon

Usage:
  • Marks weak mobs (<25% HP) with gray puff
  • Sneak to warp to nearest weak mob and execute (range 10 blocks)
  • Execute cooldown: 11s against Totem holders or Holy Protection buff
Tips:
  • Prioritizes nearest weak mob
  • Great for chaining executions
  • Use carefully in multiplayer to avoid accidental ally kills
  • Execute cannot be dodged

Starless Scythe

Sweeping Healing Obtaining:
  • End City Ominous Vaults
Usage:
  • Sweeping attack deals 60% base damage
  • Heals player for 5% damage dealt
  • Efficiency: cripples enemies
  • Fortune: increases healing 2% per level
  • Silk Touch: regenerates 3s on kill, extended if active
Tips:
  • Support weapon; use with health-draining builds
  • Fortune for instant healing; Silk Touch for sustained regen
  • Great against bosses with heavy damage

The Beginning & The End

Teleport Spirit Obtaining:
  • End City Ominous Vaults & End Dungeon Chests
  • Void fishing through Ender Crate
Usage:
  • 50% chance to summon Spirit on damage
  • Look at Spirit to teleport (0.5s)
  • AOE slash around Spirit: 5HP damage + 100% armor penetration
  • Grants 4HP Absorption, 3HP damage, +35% speed for 4s
  • Stack with Strength & Swiftness potions
Tips:
  • Pair with Sharanga for long-range Spirit attacks
  • Only one Spirit in 4-block radius
  • Teleport distance limit: 70 blocks

Harvester

Scaling Damage Tiered Obtaining:
  • Strays in End Ice biomes (1% drop)
  • Frozen Crates during Void Fishing (10% chance)
Usage:
  • Kills increase Harvester damage
  • 4 Tiers: T0-T4, attack speed decreases, movement penalty increases
  • Tier 4: Unbreakable, max damage, 20% speed penalty
Tips:
  • Grind mobs to reach max damage quickly
  • Use Swiftness to counter movement penalty
  • Anima Conduit strongest survivability option
  • Not compatible with Sharpness; Smite or Critical Strike recommended

Prismember

Spin Attack Armor Penetration Obtaining:
  • 25% drop from Empress of Light
Usage:
  • Crouch 0.4s → fiery spin: 8HP damage + 30% armor penetration
  • Applies Prismatic Inferno for 3s (+1s per Fire Aspect level)
  • Cooldown: 1 second
Tips:
  • Hit & run weapon
  • Effective against armored enemies

Kaleidoscope

Charge Explosion Obtaining:
  • Drop from Empress of Light (Overworld day)
Usage:
  • Charges over 7.5s; being hurt reduces 50%
  • Discharge ≥33 charge: heavy splash damage + fire
  • Splash: 70% near, 30% far; Fire duration scales
  • Unbreakable; immune to fire/lava/explosions
Tips:
  • Discharges inherit 50% melee damage bonuses
  • Blast Protection reduces damage, armor piercing ignores it
  • 3rd level discharges are extremely deadly

Sharanga

Ranged Obtaining:
  • Crafted with Hallowed Ingots
Usage:
  • Long-range bow
  • High projectile velocity

Call of The Void

Ranged Obtaining:
  • Before v4.0.0: Crafted at Altar of the Accursed using any bow + 8 Dragon's Breath + 16 Enderite Shards + 1 Enderite Smithing Template
  • All Voided Skeletons spawn holding a Call of The Void (0.04% drop chance)
  • /loot give @s loot stellarity:item/weapon/call_of_the_void
  • /function stellarity:_cmd/give/weapons
Usage:
  • Shoots Void Arrows, which travel 8% faster than normal arrows
  • Void Arrows shatter on impact with mobs or blocks, creating 9-11 shrapnel pieces
  • Each shrapnel deals 4HP damage and has a travel range of 3.6 blocks
  • Applies Voided I on direct hits
  • Can be enchanted with any Bow enchantment

Prismatic Punch

Ranged Obtaining:
  • 50% chance to drop from the Empress of Light in The End or during nighttime
Usage:
  • Converts any loaded projectile into a Prismatic Blast
  • Blast velocity: 16 blocks/sec, accelerates by 4.8 blocks/sec², top speed 120 blocks/sec
  • Blasts unaffected by gravity, travel straight, explode on collision with mobs/blocks
  • Explosion base damage: 9HP, Multishot doubles size & increases damage/duration of Prismatic Inferno
  • Blasts can trigger multiple explosions at the same spot; Piercing allows multiple explosions to hit same target
  • Before 4.0.0: explosions deal slightly different damage and Inferno lasts 3–8 seconds depending on enchantments
Tips:
  • Blasts are faster and more reliable than arrows for long distances
  • Area damage reduces need for perfect accuracy
  • Use regular arrows; ammo types are converted to blasts
  • Stay at safe distance to avoid blast damage

Spectral Fury

Ranged Obtaining:
  • Spectral Fury can be obtained by combining a Sharanga, 3 Diamonds, 8 Phantom Membranes, and an Enderite Upgrade Smithing Template on the Altar of The Accursed
Usage:
  • When using Spectral Arrows as ammunition, shots charged ≥1.5s will instead shoot a Wisp
  • Wisps last 7 seconds, slowly move forwards, and home in on mobs in a 16-block radius
  • Wisps can pierce 3 enemies; dissipate upon hitting the 4th
  • Wisps home in a "smart" way:
    • If 3 mobs near each other: hit all 3 one after another
    • If 2 mobs near each other: hit mob #1, then mob #2, then back to mob #1
    • If 1 mob and no other mobs nearby: hit same mob 3 times
  • Wisps deal 5 + (1.5 × arrow base damage). After piercing, damage for next hit reduces by 30% (multiplicative)
  • Power Level Damage Table:
    • 0 → 8HP / 5.6HP / 3.9HP / 2.7HP
    • 1 → 9.5HP / 6.7HP / 4.7HP / 3.3HP
    • 2 → 10.2HP / 7.1HP / 5HP / 3.5HP
    • 3 → 11HP / 7.7HP / 5.4HP / 3.8HP
    • 4 → 11.7HP / 8.2HP / 5.7HP / 4HP
    • 5 → 12.5HP / 8.8HP / 6.1HP / 4.3HP
Tips:
  • Bolts disappear after hitting a block or 3 seconds, not by distance
  • Inherits Sharanga's arrow conservation chance; Infinity is superior to Mending
  • Mending is less useful due to increased durability
  • Skeletons in The End drop Spectral Arrows when Glowing, good for mob farms

Stellar Striker

Melee Obtaining:
  • Master level trade from weaponsmiths in End Villages: 1 Gold Sword + 64 Enderite Shards
Usage:
  • Progress bar fills 0.6% per second; hitting mobs adds 2%, killing instantly adds 3%
  • Stars are gained at 10%, 20%, 45%, 70%, 100% progress
  • Sneak to fire all reached stars with slight homing: 9HP damage per star, 6HP as splash, 50% Armor Penetration
  • After shooting stars, Stellar Striker cannot gain charge for 6 seconds
  • Max damage: 45HP × 22.5 total, with 22.5HP × 11.25 penetrating armor
Tips:
  • Stars travel long distance, move decently fast, slight homing
  • Effective against high-defense enemies due to armor penetration and high damage potential

Shulker Tools

Tools / Melee Obtaining:
  • Upgrade a Netherite Tool with 4 Shulker Shells + 1 Enderite Smithing Template at the Altar of The Accursed
Usage:
  • Holding any of these items grants Haste I, allowing faster mining or 10% faster attacks
  • Useful for harvesting materials quickly or combat efficiency
Tips:
  • Shulker Sword is a good general weapon, but Stellarity provides more unique options
  • Tools are significant upgrades with no alternatives besides Hematic Pickaxe [before 4.0.0]
