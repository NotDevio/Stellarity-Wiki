Modern Player-Friendly Guide

Spellbooks

Book of Jinx

Obtaining: Crafted at the Altar of The Accursed with 1 Book, 10 Enderite Shards, and 8 Chorus Plating.
Usage: Right-click spawns a slow pink cloud for 2 seconds. Enemies hit get Jinx for 10s (-25% defense). 10s cooldown.
Tips: Great support against high-defense players.

Book of Light

Obtaining: 15% drop from Evokers (Boosted by Looting), or trade any Enchanted Book with End Village Librarians for 64 Emeralds.
Usage: Creates a healing aura (5.5 blocks radius) giving Regeneration II and Resistance I, cures Weakness & Slowness. Lasts 17s, 48s cooldown.
Tips: Excellent for multiplayer healing.

Book of Obstruct

Obtaining: Found in Stronghold library chests or from Master-level End Librarians.
Usage: Creates a wall in front that explodes after 10s.
Tips: Useful for escape or blocking enemies.

Book of Updraft

Obtaining: 15% drop from Evokers, or trade Enchanted Book with End Villagers (64 Emeralds).
Usage: Right-click gives vertical boost (~22.5 blocks) + glide for 7s. Slow Falling after glide. Sneak cancels glide. 16s cooldown.
Tips: Pair with Book of Conveyance for excellent mobility. Boosts Elytra glide ~50% stronger.

Grand Tome of Teleportation

Obtaining: Crafted at Altar of The Accursed with 1 Book, 32 Enderite Shards, 16 Ender Pearls.
Usage: Right-click casts a spark teleporting user to the first block it hits. Spark lasts 3s, can be guided. 20s cooldown.
Tips: Escape tool when out of Ender Pearls.

Tome of Return

Obtaining: Found in End City treasure chests and End Dungeons.
Usage: Hold right-click for 10s to teleport to spawn. 3 min cooldown.
Tips: Not ideal for emergency escapes due to charge time.

Shields

Copper Elektra Shield

Obtaining: 8% chance on Vindicators in Strongholds (always drops).
Usage: Charges every 7s. Sneak to release: 4 hearts damage, launches 7 blocks forward, can zap shulker bullets. 25% chance to charge creepers.
Tips: Excellent for mobility; dash helps escape or travel fast. Low damage otherwise.

Crest of The End

Obtaining: Crafted at Altar of The Accursed with 1 Shield, 1 Enderite Smithing Template, 1 Netherite Ingot.
Usage: Blocking stores damage for +15% next melee hit (up to 3 stacks). Axe block releases AoE Jinx explosion (14 damage, 4 block radius). Fire & lava immune.
Tips: Retaliation-focused; combo with Jinx for huge damage against axes.

Starstruck Shield

Obtaining: 33% drop from Empress of Light at night or in The End.
Usage: Taking damage summons 3 stars that explode for 4 hearts with 50% armor penetration. Works inside blocks and ignores immunity frames.
Tips: Synergizes with Shulker Armor for tanky, high damage output.

Other Trinkets

Enderman's Hand

Obtaining: 10% drop from Endermen.
Usage: Extends reach for breaking, placing, or interacting with blocks.

Dragon's Eye

Obtaining: Chance drop from Ender Dragon.
Usage: Makes Endermen & Phantoms passive. Grants immunity to Slowness, Weakness, Nausea, Darkness, Blindness, Mining Fatigue, and Hunger.
Tips: Convenient for ocean monuments and ignoring Phantoms.

Life Crystal

Obtaining: 66% drop from Ender Dragon.
Usage: Standing still heals via Regeneration I, increasing 1 amplifier every 2s (up to IV). Moving or being hurt resets the effect.
Tips: Swap to hold Life Crystal to heal quickly in emergencies.

Duskberry

Obtaining: Found in End Ships in The Hallow (57.14% chance). Also in Hallowed Crates.
Usage: Holding gives +12% damage, +10% speed, +4% attack speed, permanent Night Vision. Eating curses with multiple debuffs. Can plant or feed to Foxes.
Tips: Excellent for debuff achievements and exploration.

Living Flesh

Obtaining: 2.5% drop from Zombified Piglins in Flesh Tundra (Looting boosts 0.7% per level).
Usage: Slightly decreases stats, but after being hurt grants Speed II & Regeneration II for 4s.
Tips: Helps escape or recover from chip damage.

Prismatic Pearl

Obtaining: 33% drop from Empress of Light (End or Overworld night).
Usage: Functions like Ender Pearl but returns to player after 5s; flies 25% faster; leaves rainbow trail.
Tips: Useful for teleporting while tracking its path.

Soaring Insignia

Obtaining: 20% drop from Empress of Light at night or in The End; guaranteed in Overworld day.
Usage: Grants creative-like flight while sneaking; ascend/descend by looking up/down. Stops on ground, liquid, or item swap.
Tips: Good for builders and exploration; slow but precise flight.

Satchel of Voids

Obtaining: Crafted at Altar of The Accursed with 1 Bundle, 2 Nether Stars, 4 Netherite Ingots, 64 Enderite Shards, 64 Starlight Soot.
Usage: Right-click to summon temporary portable Altar for 32.55s; can place permanently on item frame attached to Crying Obsidian. 30s cooldown.

Phantom Item Frame

Obtaining: Expert-level End Villager Cartographer, 5 Enderite Shards + 1 Item Frame.
Usage: Invisible frame when item placed; emits black particles.
