💎 Stellarity Materials

Crafting components, boss materials, and upgrade items

Materials

Chorus Plating

Material

Obtaining:

  • Found inside Crates via Void Fishing
  • Found in Chests in Dungeons and Vaults in End Cities
Usage:
  • Combining 4 Chorus Plating + 1 Enderite Smithing Template + respective Netherite armor at the Altar of The Accursed crafts a piece of Champion Armor

Enderite Shard

Material

A rare shard used in advanced Stellarity crafting and trades.

Obtaining:

  • 2% chance to drop when mining End Stone
  • Found in chests and vaults of various structures

Usage

  • Craft Call of The Void: any bow + 8 Dragon's Breath + 16 Enderite Shards + 1 Enderite Smithing Template
  • Craft Tamaris (v4.0.0 recipe): Netherite Sword + Wither Skeleton Skull + 8 Enderite Shards
  • Duplicate Enderite Smithing Template: template + 9 Purpur Blocks + 5 Enderite Shards
  • Used in replacement of emeralds in End Villager trades (obtain Stellar Striker)
  • Trade with Expert End Mason for a Block of Enderite

Enderite Smithing Template

Material

Obtaining:

  • Found in End City vaults and End Dungeon chests
  • Can be duplicated with 5 Enderite Shards + 9 Purper blocks at the Altar of The Accursed
Usage:
  • Used to craft Champion Armor (4 Chorus Plating + 1 Template + Netherite armor)
  • Used to craft Hallowed Armor (4 Hallowed Ingots + 1 Template + Netherite armor)
  • Used to craft Shulker Armor/Tools (4 Shulker Shells + 1 Template + Netherite gear)
  • Used to craft Floral Armor (8 Cherry Blossom leaves + 1 Template + Netherite armor)
  • Used to craft Call of The Void (any bow + 8 Dragon's Breath + 16 Enderite Shards + 1 Template)
  • Used to craft Spectral Fury (Sharanga + 3 Diamonds + 8 Phantom Membranes + 1 Template)
  • Used to craft Hematic Pickaxe (Netherite Pickaxe + 1 Living Flesh + 1 Template)
  • All recipes crafted at the Altar of The Accursed

Hallowed Ingot

Material

Obtaining:

  • Found inside Crates fished via Void Fishing or Chests in Dungeons/Vaults in End Cities
  • Iron Ingot can be consecrated into 1 Hallowed Ingot
Usage:
  • Combining 4 Hallowed Ingots + 1 Enderite Smithing Template + respective Netherite armor at the Altar of The Accursed crafts a piece of Hallowed Armor

Starlight Soot

Material

Obtaining:

  • Pixies drop 0–4 Starlight Soot when killed
  • Found in End Ships instead of Glowstone Dust, but only if generated in The Hallow
Usage:
  • Used in crafting recipes in place of Glowstone Dust
  • Can be offered as a sacrifice at the Chapel of Light to summon the Empress of Light boss

Sand Rune

Material

Obtaining:

  • Brushing Suspicious Sand in Desert Ruins
  • Brushing Suspicious Sand in Dune Shipwrecks
  • Dropped from Ominous Vaults in End Cities (Endless Dunes biome)
Usage:
  • Used to craft the Sandstorm Trident at the Altar of the Accursed
