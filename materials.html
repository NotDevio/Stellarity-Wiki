Modern Player-Friendly Guide

Materials

Chorus Plating

Obtaining:
  • Found inside Crates via Void Fishing or Chests in Dungeons/Vaults in End Cities.
Usage:
  • Combine 4 Chorus Plating + 1 Enderite Smithing Template + netherite armor piece at Altar of the Accursed → Champion Armor

Enderite Shard

Obtaining:
  • 2% chance to drop when mining End Stone.
  • Found in chests and vaults of various structures.
Usage:
  • Craft Call of The Void, Tamaris, Enderite Smithing Template.
  • Used in End Villager trades to obtain Stellar Striker.
  • Trade with Expert End Mason for Block of Enderite.

Enderite Smithing Template

Obtaining:
  • Found in End City vaults and End Dungeon chests.
  • Can be duplicated using 5 Enderite Shards + 9 Purpur Blocks at Altar of the Accursed.
Usage:
  • Craft Champion, Hallowed, Shulker, Floral Armor.
  • Craft Call of The Void, Spectral Fury, Hematic Pickaxe at Altar of the Accursed.

Hallowed Ingot

Obtaining:
  • Found inside Crates via Void Fishing or Chests in Dungeons/Vaults in End Cities.
  • Iron Ingot can be consecrated into 1 Hallowed Ingot.
Usage:
  • Combine 4 Hallowed Ingots + 1 Enderite Smithing Template + netherite armor piece → Hallowed Armor

Sand Rune

Obtaining:
  • Brush Suspicious Sand in Desert Ruins and Dune Shipwrecks.
  • Dropped from Ominous Vaults in End Cities (Endless Dunes biome).
Usage:
  • Used to craft Sandstorm Trident at Altar of the Accursed.

Starlight Soot

Obtaining:
  • Pixies drop 0–4 Starlight Soot on death.
  • Found in End Ships in place of Glowstone Dust (only in The Hallow).
Usage:
  • Used in crafting recipes in place of Glowstone Dust.
  • Can be sacrificed at Chapel of Light to summon Empress of Light boss.

Keys

Gilded Purpur Key

Obtaining:
  • From Ominous Trial Spawners in End Cities.
Usage:
  • Right-click End City Ominous Vault to unlock it; consumes the key.

Purpur Key

Obtaining:
  • From End City Trial Spawners or by consecrating Gilded Purpur Key.
Usage:
  • Unlock End City Vaults; consumed on use.

Winged Key

Obtaining:
  • From trial spawners on End Ships or via Void Fishing.
Usage:
  • Right-click Elytra Vault to unlock it; consumes the key.

Knowledge

Endonomicon

Obtaining:
  • Drop an enchanted book over the Altar of the Accursed.
Usage:
  • Shows recipes of the Altar of the Accursed in advancement tab.
