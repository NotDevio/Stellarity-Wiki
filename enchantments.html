Enchantments & Effects

Combat Enchantments

Critical Strike: Gives 10% per level chance for an attack to deal double damage.
Source: Loot from End Cities Vaults.
Levitation Shot: Arrows have a chance to apply Levitation to targets.
Duration: Randomized, 4–7s base; each level increases min & max duration by 0.5s.
Source: Loot from End Cities.
Plated: [Duplicate of Levitation Shot; applies to Bows similarly]
Soaring: Increases Elytra glide distance by 5% per level.
Void Strike: Weapons apply Voided I to hit mobs, temporarily reducing max health by 20%.
Source: Loot from End Cities Ominous Vaults.
Void Shot: Arrows apply Voided I to hit mobs, reducing max health by 20%.
Source: Loot from End Cities Ominous Vaults.
Ambush: Deals (100 + 50 × level)% damage to mobs not actively targeting the player.

Movement Enchantments

Dune Speed: Increases movement speed while running on sandy blocks.
Blocks affected: Sand, Red Sand, Suspicious Sand, Red Concrete Powder, Gravel, Suspicious Gravel.
Source: Books buried in Desert Ruins (Suspicious Sand). Sold by Villagers in 3.0.0 (bug fixed in 4.0.0).

Technical Enchantments

Used to optimize event detection, apply special effects, or block incompatible enchantments.

Daybroken: Optimizes attack detection. Cannot be applied with Fire Aspect.
Draconic: Extra damage to mobs that cannot be punched. Incompatible with Mending and Unbreaking (4.0.0+).
Infernal Infusion: Applies Prismatic Inferno with every hit. Cannot be combined with Fire Aspect or Flame.
Mighty Wind: Optimizes throw detection. Provides built-in Loyalty III. Incompatible with most Trident enchantments.
Soul Harvest: Optimizes attack detection. Cannot be combined with Fire Aspect or Sharpness.
Vision: Provides effects that normally aren’t possible. Optimizes attribute stat bonuses.
Other Stellarity Enchantments: Power, Fortune, Spelunking, Fortitude, Wisdom, Rage, Agility.
